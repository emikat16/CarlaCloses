Common Questions

Quick answers about consultations and getting started.

How quickly will I get a response?

Carla strives to respond to all inquiries promptly, typically within one business day. For urgent matters, please call or text directly.

Is the initial consultation free?

Yes, the initial consultation to discuss your needs, goals, and explore potential loan options is complimentary.

Do you offer virtual consultations?

Absolutely. Consultations can be conducted via phone or video conference to accommodate your schedule and location.

What should I prepare for our first talk?

Having a general idea of your income, assets, debts, and your home buying or refinancing goals is helpful. Don't worry if you don't have everything – we can guide you.
